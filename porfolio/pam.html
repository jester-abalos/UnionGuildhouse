About Me

I’m Pamela Mae Sumañga. A visionary, a builder, and a woman who sees value in every detail, whether in life, design, or leadership.


Since the 5th grade, I’ve had a heart for business. What began as a simple venture into retail evolved into a creative journey that merges my love for drawing, handmade crafts, and entrepreneurship. Over time, this passion matured into a full-fledged freelance career where I bring brands to life through powerful visual storytelling.


At 18, I entered the professional world as a call center agent, a role that sharpened one of my greatest strengths, the ability to connect deeply with people. I’ve always been someone who knows how to build trust, communicate clearly, and create lasting rapport with clients and teams alike. This strength has been a key pillar in my growth as a creative professional and team leader.


Now, in my third year as a freelancer, I specialize in graphic design and digital marketing support, from crafting brand identities and marketing materials to managing social media content, designing email campaigns, and supporting SEO optimization. I approach each project with clarity, creativity, and strategy.


But I’m not just a designer, I’m also a leader. I’ve formed a team of like-minded freelancers who share my values, drive, and standard of excellence. Together, we don’t just complete projects, we build brands and cultivate client relationships that last.


Beyond work, I enjoy reading, drawing, football, badminton, and transforming imagination into handmade art. I live by a motto that has guided every step of my journey: “Vision without action is a daydream.”


Let’s collaborate and bring your vision to life with meaning, beauty, and results.
