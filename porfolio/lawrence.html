About Me

Hi! I'm Lawrence Keith Diaz, a 21-year-old Mechanical Engineering student with a passion that blends both logic and creativity. Since childhood, I’ve been fascinated by electronics and technology, always curious about how things work and how they can be improved. This early interest eventually evolved into a deep love for tech innovation, visual storytelling, and sound design.


Over the years, I’ve expanded my skills beyond engineering and into the digital creative space. I specialize in Ads Animation & Motion Graphics as well as Song Production and Audio Editing, particularly for business advertisements. Whether it’s designing smooth motion visuals or crafting audio that captivates, I bring focus, precision, and creativity into every project I handle.


When I’m not exploring new software or learning about the latest tech trends, I enjoy sharpening my mind through chess or expressing myself through music and instruments. I’m always seeking opportunities to grow, driven by curiosity and the belief that every new challenge is a chance to innovate.


Let’s create something impactful together.

Core Values: Laser-focused, opportunity-driven, and perpetually curious.
